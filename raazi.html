RAAZI

Release Date: 11 May 2018

Genre: Spy, Thriller, Drama

Duration: 2h 20min

Language: Hindi

Directed by: Meghna Gulzar

Starring: Alia Bhatt, Vicky Kaushal

"Raazi" follows a young Indian woman who marries into a Pakistani family to spy for her country during the 1971 Indo-Pak war. A moving portrayal of silent service, espionage, and courage.
