MAJOR

Release Date: 3 Jun 2022

Genre: Biographical, Action, Drama

Duration: 2h 30min

Language: Hindi, Telugu

Directed by: Sashi Kiran Tikka

Starring: Adivi Sesh, Saiee Manjrekar
