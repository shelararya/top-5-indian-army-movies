URI: The Surgical Strike

Release Date: 11 Jan 2019

Genre: Action, War, Drama

Duration: 2h 18min

Language: Hindi

Directed by: Aditya Dhar

Starring: Vicky Kaushal, Yami Gautam, Paresh Rawal

URI: The Surgical Strike is a 2019 Indian Hindi-language military film that portrays the retaliation of the Indian Army to the 2016 Uri attack. Major Vihaan Shergill leads a daring covert mission. The movie highlights the courage, patriotism, and sharp strategies of the special forces.
